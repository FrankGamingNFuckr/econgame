# 🚀 Quick Start Guide

## First Time Playing

### 1. Start the Server
```bash
python app_full.py
```

### 2. Open Browser
Go to: `http://127.0.0.1:5000`

### 3. Initial Steps
1. Click on **"Banking"** tab
2. Create either a Checking or Savings account
3. Go to **"Work"** tab
4. Click "Work" a few times to earn money (goes to Pockets)
5. Go back to **"Banking"** and deposit money into your account

## 💡 New Player Tips

### Make Your First $100,000
1. **Spam Regular Work** (10 sec cooldown, $50-$200 each)
   - Click "Work" button repeatedly
   - Money goes directly to Pockets
   
2. **Use Government Jobs** (2 min cooldown, $7,500-$30,000 each)
   - Much higher pay but longer cooldown
   - 21% taxes automatically deducted
   
3. **Check Dashboard** regularly to see total net worth

### Create Your First Business
Once you have $50,000 in pockets:
1. Go to **"Businesses"** tab
2. Enter a business name
3. Click "Create Business"
4. Hire workers ($5,000 each)
5. Workers generate $100-$500/hour automatically!

### Start Trading Stocks
1. Go to **"Stocks"** tab
2. Buy shares with money from pockets
3. Watch prices fluctuate hourly
4. Sell when prices rise!

## ⚖️ Important Mechanics

### Money Locations
- **Pockets**: Used for all purchases (stocks, crypto, items, businesses)
- **Checking**: Safe storage, easy to withdraw
- **Savings**: Earns interest hourly
- **Emergency**: Capped at $250K, provides security

### Cooldowns
- Regular Work: 10 seconds
- Government Work: 2 minutes
- Robbery: 5 minutes

### Interest & Loans
- **Savings**: Earns 3% annual interest (calculated hourly)
- **Loans**: Costs 3% DAILY interest (very expensive!)
- **Auto-Collection**: 25% of work income goes to loans automatically

## 🎯 Progression Path

### Phase 1: Worker ($0 - $100K)
- Focus on regular and government work
- Save money in checking/savings
- Don't buy anything yet

### Phase 2: Entrepreneur ($100K - $500K)
- Create 1-2 businesses
- Hire workers for passive income
- Start buying some stocks

### Phase 3: Investor ($500K - $2M)
- Own multiple businesses with many workers
- Diverse stock portfolio
- Buy housing (apartments, houses)
- Consider crypto trading

### Phase 4: Tycoon ($2M+)
- Own mansions
- Large stock/crypto portfolios
- Multiple maxed-out businesses
- Consider taking strategic loans for big investments

## ⚠️ Things to Avoid

1. **Don't take loans early** - 3% daily interest is brutal
2. **Don't keep all money in pockets** - You can be robbed!
3. **Don't buy collectibles first** - They don't generate income
4. **Don't ignore insurance** - $10K can save you from big robberies
5. **Don't forget to deposit** - Work puts money in pockets, move it to accounts!

## 🎮 Multiplayer Tips

### Playing with Friends
1. Share your player ID (visible in Admin tab)
2. Friends can rob you using your ID (risky!)
3. Buy insurance to protect against robberies
4. Compete on the Leaderboard

### Robbery Strategy
- Target players with high pocket balances
- 50% chance of success
- If caught: 2 minutes in jail
- If successful: Steal 10-30% of their pockets

## 🔧 Admin Tools (Testing)

Use the **Admin** tab to:
- **Toggle Government Shutdown**: Disable all gov work
- **Toggle Recession**: Make stocks drop faster
- **Process Hourly**: Manually trigger price updates and business revenue

## 📊 Understanding The Dashboard

The dashboard shows:
- **Total Net Worth**: Sum of all account balances
- **Businesses Owned**: Count of your businesses
- **Stock Portfolio Value**: Current value of all stocks
- **Crypto Wallet Value**: Current value of all crypto
- **Economic Status**: Active events affecting the game

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Why can't I buy stocks?**  
A: Stocks must be purchased from **Pockets**, not from checking/savings. Withdraw money first!

**Q: Where did my work money go?**  
A: It goes to **Pockets**. Check the header stats or deposit it into an account.

**Q: How do I make passive income?**  
A: Create businesses and hire workers. Go to Admin → "Process Hourly" to generate revenue.

**Q: Can I lose money?**  
A: Yes! Through:
- Loan interest (3% daily)
- Getting robbed (10-30% of pockets)
- Stock/crypto price drops
- Rent payments on housing (not yet implemented)

**Q: What's the fastest way to make money?**  
A: Government jobs pay the most ($7,500-$30,000) but have a 2-minute cooldown.
