💱 Player Trading

How Trading Works

  • Create an offer (what you give) and a request (what you want), then send it to a player by username.
  • The other player can Accept (trade executes), Decline, or ignore it until it expires.
  • Trades can include money, inventory item IDs, stocks, and crypto.
  • When a trade is accepted, the game re-checks that both players still own the assets.
  • No built-in escrow — only trade with people you trust. For big trades, split into smaller steps.

Tip: Use the inventory picker below to choose the exact item ID you own.
